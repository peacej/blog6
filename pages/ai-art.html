AI art

AI-generated art/music is the coolest thing I have ever encountered in my life.

Check out my curated list of AI art/music resources/tools. It includes a technical resources on the math and ML stuff, but also non-technical resources that anyone can use to generate art.

In 2022 August, I gave a talk on text-to-image generative models for Machine Learning Tokyo (slides) (video).

I probably will start putting stuff on artstation.com as well.
